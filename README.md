<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Mini phone book application for saving contacts of your friends, family and acquaintances.</p>

<h2>Functionalities</h2>
<p>Login and exit to your own contact page !.</p>
<p>You can do anything with your contacts:</p>

<ul>
  <li>add</li>
  <li>save</li>
  <li>delete</li>
</ul>
